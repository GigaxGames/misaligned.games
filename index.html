Built on principles, not just vibes

🔮

Innovation First

We don't copy gameplay loops. We use tech (especially ML!) to push game design into unexplored territory.

💜

Radical Respect

A welcoming space for everyone. No exceptions for "top performers." We act fast against any harassment.

🤝

Equity & Generosity

Team members own a real share of what we build. And yes, we pay our taxes where they're highest!

🎯

Optimize for Fun

We measure what keeps players engaged and follow those signals. You show us where the fun lives.

🙃

Flat Hierarchy

Good ideas come from anywhere. We judge them by one thing: does it make the game more fun?

🚀

Freedom = Profit

Valve & Riot stay independent because they're profitable. We aim for sustainability to stay free.

~ why we exist ~

Making games the right way

We started Misaligned Games for two reasons: the freedom to work on games that genuinely excite us, and to build a place where work is actually enjoyable for everyone involved.

Great games come from teams who love what they do. We're creating a space where creativity thrives, ideas flow freely, and everyone shares in what we build together. No crunch. No toxicity. Just good games.
